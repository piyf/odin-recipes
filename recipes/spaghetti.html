The Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

An Italian favorite that's beautifully simple yet bursting with flavorâ€”al dente spaghetti tossed in fragrant olive oil infused with garlic, finished with a pinch of red pepper flakes and fresh parsley. Comfort food at its most elegant.

Ingredients needed for this recipe :

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Steps to make this meal

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, then cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve half cup pasta water before draining.
  2. Peel and slice the garlic cloves thinly.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan over low heat, add sliced garlic, and cook until golden (do not burn).
  4. Stir in red pepper flakes, then add the drained spaghetti and a splash of pasta water. Toss well to coat.
  5. Chop parsley finely and sprinkle over the pasta before serving.

There is it, Your meal is ready to serve. Enjoy !
