The Green Smoothie

A vibrant, nutrient-packed blend of spinach, banana, apple, and a splash of citrus, delivering a refreshing burst of flavor and energy in every sip. A delicious way to start your day green.

Ingredients needed for this recipe :

  • Fresh spinach
  • Banana
  • Apple
  • Orange juice (or water)
  • Ice cubes

Steps to make this meal

  1. Rinse spinach thoroughly and pat dry.
  2. Peel and slice the banana. Core the apple and chop into chunks.
  3. Add spinach, banana, apple, orange juice, and ice cubes to a blender.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy. Serve immediately.

There is it, Your meal is ready to serve. Enjoy !
