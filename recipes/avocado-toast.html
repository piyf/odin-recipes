The Avocado Toast

A creamy, flavorful classic made with ripe mashed avocado spread over crisp, golden toast, topped with a sprinkle of salt, pepper, and optional extras like cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, or a drizzle of olive oil. Perfect for a quick, nourishing breakfast or snack.

Ingredients needed for this recipe :

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe of avocado
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Steps to make this meal

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Slice the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a small bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth, then season with salt, pepper, and optional lemon juice.
  4. Spread the avocado evenly over the toast, then top with halved cherry tomatoes and chili flakes if using.

There is it, Your meal is ready to serve. Enjoy !
