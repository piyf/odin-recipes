The Avocado Toast

A creamy, flavorful classic made with ripe mashed avocado spread over crisp, golden toast, topped with a sprinkle of salt, pepper, and optional extras like cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, or a drizzle of olive oil. Perfect for a quick, nourishing breakfast or snack.

Ingredients needed for this recipe :

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe of avocado
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)
