The Quinoa Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette

A light, refreshing salad featuring fluffy quinoa tossed with colorful vegetables, fresh herbs, and a zesty lemon vinaigrette. Packed with protein and nutrients, it' as satisfying as it is wholesome.

Ingredients needed for this recipe :

Quinoa Salad

  • 1 cup of quinoa
  • 2 cups of water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Lemon Vinaigrette

  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Honey or maple syrup
  • Salt & pepper

Steps to make this meal

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water in a fine-mesh sieve.
  2. Bring water or broth to a boil, add quinoa, and simmer for about 15 minutes until fluffy. Cool completely.
  3. Dice the cucumber and bell pepper. Halve the cherry tomatoes. Chop parsley finely.
  4. In a bowl, whisk olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper to make the vinaigrette.
  5. Combine quinoa, vegetables, and parsley in a large bowl. Pour over vinaigrette and toss gently.

There is it, Your meal is ready to serve. Enjoy !
